engineering

The findings of scientific inquiry and the intentionality of design are sometimes brought into form and function through engineering. Despite the fact that if I was a member of any species other than H. sapiens, contemporary biologists would certainly call the activities I described in the design and care sections “ecosystem engineering,” for the organization of this website I restrict the term engineering to my burgeoning work with software.

I built this website with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as part of a practice problem during my studies.
